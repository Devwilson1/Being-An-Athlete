Devin Wilson

CEO & Athlete

My Name is Devin Wilson and I am a 15 year black male entrepenuear who is an Athlete. I play football and baseball at the highschool level and while participating in these spots I have learned that the recruitment process is hard. That is what sparked my Idea for my buissness and I want to make it well known and used all over the country. Some more about me is that I am from Oakland, CA and I have 2 brothers. I am apart of the Hidden Genius project and I am in cohort Oak Black 9. With my buissness I want to bring awareness to the technicalities and struggles of being recruited as a high school athlete and that is what I intend to do for Athletes all around the world.
